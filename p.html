Our Story

In

Something Delicious

, we believe food is more than just fuel—it's an emotion. Founded in 2023, When Something Delicious first opened, it wasn’t the cozy, welcoming spot you see today. It began in a tiny kitchen with one stove, two tables, and a dream that barely fit in the space. The founders, Aarav and Meera, believed that great food could heal tired souls — and that belief carried them through nights when the lights almost went out and mornings when they cooked with more hope than ingredients. Every dish on the menu carries a piece of that journey. The creamy butter chicken that became our crowd favorite? It was first made during a rainy evening when the spice shelves were almost empty — a blend of improvisation, courage, and pure love for flavor. The sizzling tandoori platters came from endless weekends of experimenting until the aromas felt like home. Over the years, our little kitchen turned into a neighborhood story — a place where late-night laughter meets comfort food, where recipes are not just cooked but cared for. We’re still driven by the same belief that started it all: simple ingredients, honest effort, and a touch of heart make everything delicious. So when you walk through our doors, remember — you’re not just tasting food. You’re tasting a journey that started with struggle and grew into something delicious.

Our chefs use traditional recipes passed down through generations, combining them with modern techniques to bring you flavors that feel like home but taste like luxury.
